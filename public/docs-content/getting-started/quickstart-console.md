# Quickstart: Console

In this guide you will create your HoneyOps account, configure the base organization (Account) and
invite your first admin user.

## 1. Sign up in the console

Navigate to `https://console.honeyops.test` and complete the sign-up form with your name, company
email and country.

## 2. Verify your email

Check your inbox for the verification link. Until the email is verified, access to the console will
be limited.

## 3. Review your Account and billing profile

Open the **Profile → Company** tab and fill in your billing information (company name, tax ID and
address). This will be used for invoicing and partner discounts.
